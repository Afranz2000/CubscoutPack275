Welcome to Pack 275!

We're a Cub Scout pack serving families in Manhattan Beach and the surrounding area. Our pack is dedicated to helping boys and girls in grades K–5 develop character, citizenship, and a love of adventure through fun activities, community service, and the great outdoors.

Whether you're a current Scout family or just exploring whether Scouting is right for your child — we're glad you're here. Feel free to reach out to any of our leaders below, or come visit us at a meeting!

About Cub Scouting

Cub Scouting is a program of Scouting America for children in Kindergarten through 5th grade (ages 5–10). The program focuses on fun, family, and personal development through age-appropriate activities, community service, and outdoor adventures.

Scouts progress through ranks as they complete requirements and earn achievements. Each rank corresponds to a grade level:

🦁 Lion (Kinder) 🐯 Tiger (1st Grade) 🐺 Wolf (2nd Grade) 🐻 Bear (3rd Grade) ⚙️ Webelos (4th Grade) 🏔️ Arrow of Light (5th Grade)

For more information about the Cub Scout program, visit Scouting.org.

Ready to Join Pack 275?

We welcome new Scouts of all backgrounds and experience levels. If your child is in Kindergarten through 5th grade, they're eligible to join! Come to any of our Pack meetings as a guest — no commitment required. Or reach out to our Cubmaster to learn more.
